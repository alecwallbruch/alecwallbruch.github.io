My Student Life

I first enrolled in UW-Madison as a Mechanical Engineer, but I quickly realized I was better suited to be a Statistics major, which I will now graduate as. I have had a difficult 3 years at Wisconsin, but every time I take a walk around campus I remember why I work hard to stay at this beautiful school.

Activities I'm Involved In

When I first came to school I did not intend on joining greek-life. It was not until I felt extremely lonely in the dorms that I decided to rush. I'm now a part of Beta Theta Pi and I've made some of the best friends I could have ever asked for. I also partake in many intramural sports to keep myself active and retain my competitive spirit.

My personal favorite activity is Humorology. Humo is a variety show put on by casts made entirely of students within greek-life, with the main goal being to raise as much money as possible for the selected charity that year. I had the pleasure of being a director of my cast sophomore year and it was incredible. While we didn't get to perform our show last year, my cast freshman year was able to.
