My Hometown!

A sunset seen from my backyard

I live in a massive city in Illinois!

Aurora, Illinois has a population of almost 200,000 people, making it second to only Chicago. As you might have guessed because I live in a massive city, my high school was also ginormous! I went to school with over 2,700 students, 860 of which were in my class!

Me in front of my high school

Why I love my city so much

Growing up only a 30 minute drive away from Chicago was awesome. I got to experience life growing up in the suburbs while being so close to the best city in the world. It was only once I left my city did I realize how much I love it. I get to root for the Cubs, and in 2016, I was rewarded with the best perk a Chicagoland citizen can get: a Cubs World Series win.
