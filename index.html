Where I'm from and what I do in life

I'm from Aurora, Illinois, a large suburb west of Chicago. I've lived there all my life until I moved to Madison, Wisconsin for school. I study Statistics at the University of Wisconsin and want to pursue a career in sociology involving statistics.

What I'm passionate about

As you might be able to guess, I'm very passionate about social science and the study of populations of people. My more interesting passions are going on vacations and studying philosophy; and finally, my most controversial passion is for politics.
